AL-ANDALUS BIMEX

Al-Andalus BIMEX has an extensive line of valves suited for water, air, and wastewater.

Al-Andalus BIMEX is a major industrial company headquartered in Cairo, Egypt. Since its foundation 25+ years ago, it has been a powerhouse in the sectors of manufacturing valves, fire hydrants, anti-water hammer systems, compact wastewater treatment plants, bio media and metallic tanks.

Our outmost goals are the satisfaction of our customers and meeting their every needs. Hence Al-Andalus BIMEX is at the forefront of market technology and practices. Thus, and during it is life span, Al-Andalus BIMEX volume of work has exceeded 8 billion Egyptian pounds, with a portfolio of projects in international markets spanning MENA region and Africa.
